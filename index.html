AGR FAIR Assessment Results

This table summarizes FAIR assessment results for each of the members of the Alliance of Genome Resources (AGR). AGR consists of resources from Flybase, Wormbase, The Gene Ontology Consortium, The Saccharomyces Genome Database, The Zebrafish Information Network, The Rat Genome Database, and Mouse Genome Informatics. FAIR assessments were performed using FAIRshake and the results can be accessed within the AGR project. Summary images for each analysis are available within the table below and complete analysis reports can be accessed at the bottom of the page in the form of a Jupyter Notebook.
